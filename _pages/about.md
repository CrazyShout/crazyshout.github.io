I am Yunjiang Xu (徐云江), a PhD student at [City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/), supervised by Prof. [Jianping Wang](https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk/~jianwang/) and Prof. Qian Zhou. I received my M.S. degree from Soochow University in 2025, supervised by Prof. Jin Wang and Prof. Lingzhi Li.

My research interests include **autonomous driving**, **collaborative perception**, **embodied intelligence**, and **unsupervised learning**. I focus on making perception systems robust in real-world multi-agent settings — handling asynchrony, bandwidth constraints, and occlusion through principled feature-level fusion.

For collaboration or academic exchange, please feel free to reach out via email.
